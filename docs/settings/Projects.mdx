Projects
--------

## Overview

Projects allow you to easily monitor your performance across multiple cloud architectures and applications. Projects can be used to organize modules (Dashboard, Infrastructure Monitoring, APM, Logs, RUM, Synthetic Monitoring, Recommendations) in an isolated and controlled environment.

### Limitations

#### Usage

Data is shared across all Projects and visible to all Project members. You must download the specific modules for your new Project in order to monitor performance. You can choose to view all Projects Usage at once, or per Project.

You will be billed for your entire Middleware Account, which includes all of your active Projects.
