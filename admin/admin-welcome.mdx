Administration is designed specifically for system administrators to ensure their tasks are executed with precision and efficiency. Each section transparently provides all of the necessary guides and tools for users to maintain a seamless and well-organized observability environment.

## Getting Started with Settings

[Advanced](/admin/settings/advanced_settings): Disable monitoring and collecting data to edit directory paths for logging

[API Keys](/admin/settings/api_keys): Access and create your API keys

[Legal and Compliance](/admin/settings/legal_compliance): Review Middleware's Terms and Conditions and Security policies

[Plans and Billing](/admin/settings/plans_billing): Reveiw and purchase Middleware's subscription plans

[Profile](/admin/settings/profile):  View your Profile status and change your Account information

[Projects](/admin/settings/Projects): Separate your observability stack across distinct projects

[Teams](/admin/settings/teams): Create a new Team within your organization

[Usage](/admin/settings/usage): Monitor your usage over any timeframe and selected projects

[Users](/admin/settings/users): Add and track new Users to your organization
