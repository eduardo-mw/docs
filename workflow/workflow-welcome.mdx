Workflows provide a comprehensive guide to leveraging the full power of Middleware's observability platform and tools. Each Workflow provides a detailed explanation and step-by-step guide to configuring the best workflow for your team.

## Getting Started with Workflows

[Alerts](/workflow/alerting/setup-alert): Create, monitor, filter and maintain custom notifications for your entire organization

[APM](/workflow/apm/apm-dashboard): View Services, Databases, Traces, and Continuous Profiling in the APM Dashboard

[Dashboard Builder](/workflow/custom-dashboard/build-custom-dashboard): Create drag and drop dashboard with customizable widgets and filters

[Infrastructure Monitoring](/workflow/infrastructure-monitoring/container-monitoring): Monitor hosts, Kubernetes clusters, and Docker containers

[Log Monitoring](/workflow/log-monitoring/logs): Monitor and filter logging data across your organization's entire platform

[Synthetic Monitoring](/workflow/synthetic-monitoring/synthetic-monitoring): Simulate system-wide interactions with multistep synthetic monitoring
