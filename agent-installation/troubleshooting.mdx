It may take a few moments before you start seeing metrics appear when you first install the agent, the time can vary depending on host type and size of cluster. If you do not see metrics in the <a href="https://docs.middleware.io/docs/unified-dashboard/unified-view" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank" translate="no">Unified View</a> after a minute or two take a look at the list below for common fixes. Our team is also available to assist in the Slack channel that was created for you at sign up.

### Troubleshooting steps:
