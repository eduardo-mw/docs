The ClickHouse Integration allows users to effortlessly ingest and monitor their column-oriented ClickHouse data. Use our default dashboard to understand system-wide usage patterns and identify ClickHouse cluster performance bottlenecks. Understand how your high-volume SQL queries perform in real-time to further maximize your infrastructure's efficiency.

# Prerequisites

1. Middleware Host agent must be installed on your machine
